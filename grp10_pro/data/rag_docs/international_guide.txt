INTERNATIONAL STUDENT GUIDE

1. Arrival:
- Report to International Office within 48 hours
- Submit passport and visa copies

2. Visa Rules:
- Maintain valid student visa
- Inform authorities before travel

3. Accommodation:
- Hostel available for international students
- Private housing also allowed

4. Fees:
- Fees may differ for international students
- Payment must be in USD or equivalent

5. Support Services:
- Counseling support available
- Language assistance programs

6. Cultural Integration:
- Participate in university events
- Respect local culture

7. Emergency Contacts:
- International office helpline available 24/7

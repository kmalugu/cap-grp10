LIBRARY RULES

1. Timings:
- 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM (Weekdays)
- 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Weekends)

2. Borrowing:
- Max 3 books per student
- Duration: 14 days

3. Fines:
- ₹5 per day after due date

4. Conduct:
- Silence must be maintained
- No eating inside library

5. Digital Library:
- Students can access e-books and journals

6. ID Requirement:
- Library card mandatory for entry

7. Lost Books:
- Must be replaced or paid for

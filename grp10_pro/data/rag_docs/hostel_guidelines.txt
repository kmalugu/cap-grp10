HOSTEL GUIDELINES

1. Timings:
- Entry allowed till 10:00 PM.
- Late entry requires prior permission.

2. Room Rules:
- No switching rooms without approval.
- Keep rooms clean and hygienic.

3. Visitors:
- Visitors allowed only in common areas.
- Visiting hours: 5 PM – 8 PM.

4. Discipline:
- No loud music after 10 PM.
- No alcohol or drugs allowed.

5. Safety:
- Electrical appliances must be approved.
- Report issues to warden immediately.

6. Mess Rules:
- Follow mess timings strictly.
- Avoid food wastage.

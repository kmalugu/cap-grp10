PLACEMENT PREPARATION GUIDELINES

1. Eligibility:
- Minimum CGPA: 6.5
- No active backlogs

2. Preparation Steps:
- Learn DSA (Data Structures & Algorithms)
- Practice coding on platforms like LeetCode
- Prepare aptitude and reasoning

3. Resume Guidelines:
- Keep it 1 page
- Highlight projects and internships

4. Placement Process:
- Online test
- Technical interview
- HR interview

5. Important Rules:
- Students can apply to multiple companies
- Once selected, must follow placement policy

6. Soft Skills:
- Communication is very important
- Practice mock interviews

7. Internship Conversion:
- Good performance in internship can lead to PPO

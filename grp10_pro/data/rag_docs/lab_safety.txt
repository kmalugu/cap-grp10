LAB SAFETY GUIDELINES

1. General Rules:
- Follow instructor instructions strictly
- No unauthorized experiments

2. Dress Code:
- Wear lab coat where required
- Closed shoes mandatory

3. Equipment Handling:
- Handle devices carefully
- Report damage immediately

4. Electrical Safety:
- Do not touch wires with wet hands
- Switch off devices after use

5. Emergency:
- Know fire exit routes
- Inform lab assistant immediately

6. Cleanliness:
- Keep workspace clean
- Dispose waste properly
